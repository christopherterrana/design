Meadows Museum

Meadows Museum is a spanish historical museum with artworks dating from the 10th and 21st century. The interior is full of spanish art originating from outside of Spain, from paintings, to artifacts, to sculptures, there are many different forms of artistic history on display. Personally speaking it was fascinating to walk around the museum and take pictures of everything I possibly could so I can appreciate the art anytime I want to reminisce about my trip to Texas.

Katy Trail

The Katy trail is a 3.5 mile paved walking and bike path for people to relax and take in the fresh air. Especially in Texas where there aren’t many areas for pedestrians to get around without a vehicle.
